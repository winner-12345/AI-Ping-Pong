AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop on a stable surface
  2. Stay approx 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on the screen
  4. Now press the play button and move away from the screen
  5. The red paddle is your paddle and will move like your right wrist
